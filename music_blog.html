Persevere With Positivity

This week we are reminded to keep calm and think positively.

november 2016

october 2016

Chicago-based Whitney landed the top spot on this month's Top Picks from WCBN. Melodic and distinct, this group is definitely one to watch. Their debut album, Light Upon the Lake, features 10 tracks, each equally enjoyable.

Best New Music goes to Solange, and it wasn't even close (sorry, Green Day). Truly emerging from the shadow of her sister for the first time, A Seat at the Table was an opportunity to prove herself. And she did. Cranes in the Sky features her divine vocals driven by a sweet bass line.

THE name in West Coast jazz, Kamasi Washington, played two nights at the Michigan Theater this past week. Gaining attention on Kendrick Lamar's To Pimp a Butterfly, Washington went on to release The Epic and more recently, the Kipling Theme on Netflix's star-studded soundtrack for its new series, The Get Down.

We also give a nod to Mark Mothersbaugh of DEVO, recent guest of the Penny W. Stamps Speaker Series, and U-M alum Theo Katzman of Vulfpeck who will be playing Nov 5th at the Blind Pig.
